Microphones and Bocals

Microphones can give the most accurate sound, but sometimes there are issues with feedback. Most people use two microphones, one at the bell for the lowest notes, and one aimed towards the left hand fingers. This blend does well to capture all notes of the bassoon. Bocals pick up all notes more or less evenly, but they don't sound the same as an acoustic bassoon. The telex pickup has a strong signal, but is a bit honky. The little jake pickup requires a preamp, and is a bit heavy in the bass. A volume pedal is advised for this setup to help with the reduced dynamic range of a pickup. romine13-equipment It is important with microphones to understand the type of microphone and polar pattern to avoid feedback issues. grew14-microphones
