Why do we have inversions?

Smooth out the bass line, interplay with upper voices

Inversions are often more on the embellishing chord side of the spectrum

    \version "2.24.3"

        \new Staff{
          \relative c' {

              {\clef bass
               c4( bes |
               ees4. des8 |
               c ees aes bes |

              \\

               g bes g bes 
              }
            >>
          }
        }
        \new Staff{

          \time 2/4
          \key aes \major

            aes4( des |
            c g |
            aes8 g f f' |
