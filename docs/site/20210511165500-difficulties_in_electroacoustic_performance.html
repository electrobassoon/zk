Difficulties in Electroacoustic Performance

There are many difficulties a performer has to deal with when performing electroacoustic music.

One is the proximity of microphones, cables, and limiting a performer's normal expressive movements. bullock13_difficulties

Sometimes electroacoustic pieces can put additional strain on your body and physical health. enns17-difficulties

Another, in some pieces, is the inflexibility of the accompanist of fixed media. mcnutt03-prisoner

The notation of electronic music is not standard and does not seem to have a solution for all situations. pestova18-notation Notation can be unhelpful. Hearing the electronics on stage can pose difficulties. The physical differneces in each performer make some setups difficult. mcnutt03-difficulties

Using speakers poses other problems. They are directional and concentrated, instead of diffuse like acoustic performers. Mixing the two is a difficulty. morrill81_difficulties, pennycook97-difficulties

Additionally, there is a perceptual difficulty for listeners. Only one audio source is seen, yet several are heard. frengel10-difficulties Audiences also need to discard previous conceptions of computer music as things have changed a lot. belet03-audiences

Another difficulty arises from the knowledge of the teacher. At times, the student may know more about the technology than the teacher does. It takes considerable time and effort for the teacher to learn new skills and develop new teaching resources. brown07-difficulties, pennycook97-education behr14-difficulties masone16-difficulties

Halls are not desgned for electroacoustic music, but for traditional music. stockhausen96-halls, pennycook97-difficulties, kimura03_acoustics A performance can be bad because of spatialization, eq, etc as much as wrong notes. hoffman13-ideal

Dynamics can be a large issue for the bassoon, especially when using electric bocals. romine13-difficulties behr14-difficulties

Every piece seems to require purchasing some new form of equipment, or has a new technical setup. romine13-difficulties

There is a balance of sound to feedback with microphones and pickups romine13-difficulties

The cost of the equipment is a major issue for performers (the ones that do the most for the genre to survive), and many performance spaces don't want to spend money on things that won't get used. It's a vicious circle.pennycook97-difficulties

Similarly, institutions are not funding, and thus not encouraging, the development of electroacoustic music. waters16_economy

Controllers can be distracting to the audience and the music. kimura03_stage_appearance

However, music playing is already frought with difficulties and should be, or it would not be satisfying mcdermott13-difficulty That is why we play instruments dixon06-play

Pieces go out of date, and if one piece of a system is gone, the whole piece needs to be rebuilt by the composer. canazza01-preservation Some composer are more willing to do this than others. grew14-difficulties

Hearing the eletronics on stage can be difficult and cause syncing issues. behr14-difficulties

The bassoon itself makes amplification and pitch tracking more difficult perhaps than other instruments. masone16-difficulties Many effects units are designed for guitar which has a clear tone, unlike wind instruments. rothenberg10-tone
