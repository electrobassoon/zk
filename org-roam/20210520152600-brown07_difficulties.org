#+title: brown07-difficulties
#+filetags: :teacherstudentrelationship:

One difficulty with incorporating computer music into the curriculum is the time and effort to learn new skills as a teacher. Often, the student will know more about the technology than the teacher does. Here the role of the teacher is to guide the student in learning opportunities, at times becoming a learner alongside the student.
